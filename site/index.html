Why everything runs in your browser

Every tool on this site uses client-side JavaScript libraries to do the actual conversion work, so the Markdown or HTML you paste in never leaves your machine. There's no upload step, no account, and no server that could log or retain your content — which matters if you're pasting anything from an internal doc, a draft you haven't published yet, or output from an AI assistant you'd rather not send to a third party. It also means the tools work instantly: no network round trip, no waiting on a queue, just a live conversion as you type.
