About __SITE_NAME__

__SITE_NAME__ is a small collection of single-purpose Markdown tools — converters, cleaners, and generators — built to do one job each and do it without asking you to upload anything. Paste your Markdown or HTML in, get the result back, and nothing you typed ever crosses the network.

That constraint is deliberate. A lot of the Markdown people convert day to day — draft docs, internal notes, AI-assistant output — isn't something they want sitting on a third-party server, even briefly. Doing the conversion entirely in the browser with open libraries like marked and DOMPurify means there's no upload step to worry about in the first place.

The site is static, has no backend, and no account system. Questions, bug reports, or tool requests are welcome via GitHub.
